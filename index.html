How Does Dropshipping Work?

1) Customer Places Order On Your Store.

You will be notified every time a customer buys something from your store, you will have an overview of all sales data from within your store dashboard.

2) You Place Order With Your Supplier.

Once a customer places an order on your website you'll be able to order that same product with your supplier at wholesale pricing using our easy one click fulfilment.

3) Your Supplier Ships Product To Customer.

Your supplier will ship the product right to the address they entered on your order form, totally bypassing the need for you to hold any inventory.
